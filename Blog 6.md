# Blog 6: Final Improvements
Most of the game mechanics were already finished by this point. And rather than adding new mechanics improving small details was deemed more useful. The way matches start was changed. As at the start of each match the tournament announcer says who fights and gives us some small description in order for the player and enemies to wait for the announcement to be over a MatchManager was made. It is in charge of initially calling the methods to play the announcements, activating the player and enemy controllers and playing victory, defeat voice lines as well as deactivating events if the match is over before it activates. Of course to keep it single responsibility it mostly calls the public methods from other scripts to achieve the aforementioned actions.

Further improvements have been made in what the enemies say during the battle as now every now and then they same a line in mostly humoristic tone, in line with why they are in the match. As all of the voicelines are custom a generative AI ( ElevenLabs) was used to make them instead of some asset packs. To make things more realistic additional sound effects were used like hitting the enemy or when defending from their attacks.

Finally, we also added a skybox to enhance the realism and mood of the game. This was one of the easier tasks in the project. We just needed to go to the window for rendering, select lighting and then go to the environment section. From there we only needed to select a skybox material which was one the assets from unity store that we used.
